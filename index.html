About PrimaBlock

PrimaBlock started as a passion project to learn about smart contracts on Ethereum. Our focus is on automating the accounting and remittance work involved in managing pools through the use of smart contracts.

Using PrimaBlock, you can deploy a smart contract on the Ethereum network. The person who deploys the smart contract is called the "pool creator." The smart contract can only be administered by the pool creator and anyone else nominated by the pool creator. In other words, the pool creator has custody of the funds.

Licensing our smart contract technology reduces the risk of accounting and remittance errors. Also, the smart contracts ensure that the custody of the funds is held by the pool creator.
